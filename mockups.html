What Each Direction Is Testing

Coastal Atlas

Leads with geography and makes the coast feel browsable. Strong for future maps, cams, custom spots, and bank tuning.

Signal Dashboard

Prioritises quick decisions, rankings, and condition factors. Strongest for a daily “where should I surf?” workflow.

Surf Logbook

Adds a more human read and local voice. Strong if you want less raw data and more useful interpretation.

Tide & Timeline

Organises the day around windows. Strong if timing, tide, wind shift, and spot rotation are the real product.
